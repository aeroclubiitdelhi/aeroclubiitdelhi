# About Us

The **AeroClub, IIT Delhi** is a student-run technical club dedicated to promoting innovation in **Aeromodelling, UAVs, Drones, Autonomous Flight, and Aerospace Engineering**.

Our objective is to provide students with hands-on experience through projects, workshops, competitions, and research, enabling them to transform theoretical knowledge into practical engineering solutions.

Whether you're passionate about aircraft design, robotics, AI, embedded systems, control engineering, or computer vision, Aero Club offers opportunities to learn, build, and fly.
